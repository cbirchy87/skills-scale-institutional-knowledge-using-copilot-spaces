## Crisis Communication Plan

### Trigger Conditions
- [ ] Production outage affecting customers
- [ ] Major security incident
- [ ] Critical blocker jeopardizing release
- [ ] Significant scope or timeline change
- [ ] Key team member departure

### Crisis Communication Process
1. **Immediate Response** (within 1 hour):
   - Communications Lead notified by Project Manager
   - Assess situation with Risk Manager and technical leads
   - Notify affected stakeholders with initial alert
   
2. **Ongoing Updates** (every 2-4 hours until resolved):
   - Status updates on progress
   - Impact assessment
   - Expected resolution timeline
   
3. **Resolution Communication**:
   - Confirm issue is resolved
   - Summary of impact and actions taken
   - Schedule post-mortem/retrospective
