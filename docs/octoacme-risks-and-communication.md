## Purpose
Explain how to identify, manage, and communicate risks and dependencies across the project lifecycle. This document outlines responsibilities for the Risk Manager and Communications Lead in coordination with the Project Manager.

## Role Accountabilities
- **Risk Manager**: Owns the risk register, facilitates risk assessments, and monitors mitigation plans
- **Communications Lead**: Develops communication plans, coordinates stakeholder updates, and manages information flow
- **Project Manager**: Coordinates overall delivery and partners with both Risk Manager and Communications Lead

## Risk Register
Maintain a simple table with:
- ID
- Description
- Impact (High/Med/Low)
- Likelihood (High/Med/Low)
- Owner
- Mitigation plan
- Status

## Risk Lifecycle
- **Identify**: Risk Manager facilitates risk identification during planning and ongoing execution with input from all team members
- **Assess**: Risk Manager estimates impact and likelihood with subject matter experts
- **Mitigate**: Risk Manager tracks mitigation actions with assigned owners; escalates as needed
- **Monitor**: Risk Manager reviews risks at weekly syncs with Project Manager and updates status
- **Communicate**: Risk Manager coordinates with Communications Lead to ensure appropriate stakeholder awareness

## Stakeholder Communication
- **Communications Lead** identifies stakeholder groups and their communication needs (e.g., engineering, sales, support, leadership)
- **Communications Lead** develops communication plans with schedules and appropriate channels
- **Project Manager** and **Communications Lead** provide regular updates (weekly or milestone-based)
- Use a single source of truth (project README or release doc) for status, maintained by the **Project Manager**
- **Risk Manager** provides risk summaries for inclusion in stakeholder communications

## Communication Templates
**Communications Lead** maintains and customizes these templates for project needs.

Weekly Status Template (prepared by **Project Manager**, distributed by **Communications Lead**):
- Progress this week:
- Next steps:
- Risks & blockers (from **Risk Manager**):
- Ask / decisions needed:

Incident Communication (coordinated by **Communications Lead**):
- Triage summary
- Actions being taken
- Expected timeline
- Post-incident blameless retrospective scheduled

## Escalation Paths
Escalations are managed by the **Project Manager** in coordination with the **Risk Manager**:
- Level 1: Team-level -> PM -> Product Lead
- Level 2: PM -> Sponsor with risk assessment from **Risk Manager**
- For security incidents, follow the security incident runbook and notify Security on-call; **Communications Lead** manages external communications
